
## **Project Outcomes**

##  Overview
The implementation of the AI-powered diagnostic support system resulted in measurable improvements in operational efficiency, diagnostic accuracy, and user adoption.

---

##  Key Metrics Achieved

| Metric | Before | Target | Achieved |
|--------|--------|--------|----------|
| Turnaround Time | 48 hrs | 36 hrs | 30 hrs |
| Diagnostic Accuracy | 85% | 95% | 93% |
| Adoption Rate | 0% | 80% | 85% |
| Error Rate | 12% | 5% | 6% |

---

##  Operational Impact
- Reduced turnaround time by ~25%, enabling faster report delivery  
- Improved workflow efficiency through AI-assisted pre-analysis  
- Reduced manual workload for lab technicians  

---

##  Clinical Impact
- Enhanced diagnostic accuracy and consistency  
- Supported early detection through AI-assisted insights  
- Reduced risk of human error  

---

##  Business Impact
- Increased patient satisfaction due to faster results  
- Improved lab throughput and scalability  
- Strengthened competitive positioning through AI adoption  

---

## People Impact
- Reduced workload stress for technicians  
- Increased confidence in diagnostic processes  
- Successful adoption of new technology with minimal resistance
