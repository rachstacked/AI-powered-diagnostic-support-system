
#  Lessons Learned

##  Overview
This project provided key insights into managing technology-driven healthcare implementations, particularly around change management, stakeholder alignment, and system validation.

---

##  Key Learnings

### 1. Change Management is Critical
Early resistance from lab technicians highlighted the importance of involving end users from the beginning.

**Action Taken:**
- Conducted early demos and pilot testing  
- Incorporated user feedback into system improvements  

---

### 2. AI Requires Human Validation
AI outputs were not fully reliable in initial stages.

**Action Taken:**
- Introduced a validation layer with pathologists  
- Improved trust and accuracy over time  

---

### 3. Integration is More Complex Than Expected
Technical challenges during API integration caused minor delays.

**Action Taken:**
- Broke integration into smaller milestones  
- Increased coordination with IT and vendor teams  

---

### 4. Communication Drives Alignment
Different stakeholders required different communication styles.

**Action Taken:**
- Tailored updates (technical vs business)  
- Maintained regular sync-ups  

---

### 5. Data-Driven Monitoring Improves Decisions
Tracking KPIs helped identify improvement areas quickly.

**Action Taken:**
- Used dashboard insights to guide corrective actions
