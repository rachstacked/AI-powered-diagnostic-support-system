##  Background
The diagnostic laboratory is experiencing increased patient load, leading to delays in report turnaround time (TAT) and a higher probability of manual diagnostic errors. Existing workflows rely heavily on manual interpretation, making scalability difficult.
To address this, the organization plans to implement an AI-powered diagnostic support system that assists technicians in analysis and improves efficiency.

---

## Project Objectives 
 * Reduce report turnaround time by 25% within 3 months of implementation
 * Improve diagnostic accuracy by 15%
 * Achieve 80% adoption rate among lab technicians within 2 months
 * Red uce manual workload by 30%

---

## **Scope**
**✅ In Scope:**
* AI vendor selection and onboarding
* Integration with existing lab systems
* Staff training and onboarding
* Pilot testing and phased rollout
* Performance monitoring
  
**❌ Out of Scope:**
* Development of AI model from scratch
* Multi-location deployment (future phase)
* Full automation (human validation remains)
