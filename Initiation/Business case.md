## Problem Statement
The lab currently faces inefficiencies due to manual diagnostic processes, resulting in delayed reports, inconsistent accuracy, and limited scalability.

---

## Proposed Solution
Implement an AI-powered diagnostic support system that assists technicians in identifying patterns and anomalies, thereby improving speed and accuracy.

---

## Benefits

  ## Tangible Benefits:
* Faster report generation → increased throughput
* Reduced rework due to fewer errors
* Operational cost optimization
 ## Intangible Benefits:
* Improved patient trust
* Enhanced reputation of the lab
* Better employee productivity & reduced burnout

 ---

## Cost vs Benefit Insight
While initial investment in AI integration and training is significant, the long-term gains in efficiency, accuracy, and scalability outweigh the costs.

---

## Risks of NOT doing this project
* Continued delays and inefficiencies
* Increased competition from AI-enabled labs
* Staff burnout
* Decline in patient satisfaction

----

## Recommendation
Proceed with a phased implementation using a hybrid project management approach to balance compliance and flexibility.
